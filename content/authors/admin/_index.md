Dr. Umang Bhatt is an Assistant Professor in Trustworthy Artificial Intelligence at the University of Cambridge and its Centre for Human-Inspired AI. He is also a Fellow in Computer Science at King's College, Cambridge.

Dr. Bhatt leads the TRACE Lab, which focuses on building trustworthy artificial intelligence systems for high-stakes settings like healthcare, education, and public safety. The lab develops new algorithms and tools that support effective human-AI interaction and manage the partnership between humans and AI agents.
